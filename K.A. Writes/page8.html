he gazed at her, their eyes met by chance,
a wistful look on both faces, a shared longing for the ephemeral idyllic times.
as we live through our paths, this capricious nostalgia made me enamoured even more,
although you've changed from who you were back then, the "you" I knew was a scintillating light of my past.

what happened?

why did you let it end in such a complex conundrum?
those enigmatic laughs and smiles, or even your actions—a lachrymose farewell, our last.
