I took a picture of you, a fleeting image—an effervescent,\nas the dawn starts to move, the silhouette begins to leave its fringe,\na clearer, luminous you are, hearing the tintinnabulation of the bells inside my mind,\n your beauty was ethereal, as if you were a paragon of those in fairytales I've dreamed.\n\nI can tell by the eyes, the conviviality you felt, the strange atmosphere, and the yellow scenery,\n a golden sea, where its waves catch and reflect your light,\na sun sets, knowing—it will be my first and last image of you—a once in a lifetime sight.
