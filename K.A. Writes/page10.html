months passed—look at you right now,
you're brighter than the last time we've been,
did I make a mistake in leaving you that day?
or did I just make the best choice I've had since it may,
glad to see you again, my luna.

more likely that I'm addicted to sighting your gleam as you're at your fullest,
watching you shine, knowing that this will be the time—the only thing I can do to keep you inside,
please take care of yourself, as I'm not capable of reaching you from this distance.

I'll just admire you from afar,
I'll treasure what's only ours, my big star.
